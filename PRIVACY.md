# Privacy Policy for Unchained Vibes - for Claude

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

## Overview
Unchained Vibes respects your privacy. We collect zero data from users.

## What We Collect
Nothing. We collect absolutely no data.

## What We Access
To function, this extension requires:
- **Microphone Access** - To capture your voice using browser's Web Speech API
- **Storage Permission** - To save your custom trigger phrase locally on your device
- **Claude.ai Access** - To insert text into Claude's chat interface

## What We Don't Do
- ❌ We don't record your voice
- ❌ We don't store your conversations
- ❌ We don't send any data to external servers
- ❌ We don't use cookies or tracking
- ❌ We don't collect analytics

## How Your Data is Processed
All speech recognition is handled by your browser's built-in Web Speech API. Your voice never leaves your device.

When you speak, your browser converts speech to text. That text is inserted into Claude's input box - the same as if you had typed it. Your conversation with Claude follows Claude/Anthropic's privacy policy.
